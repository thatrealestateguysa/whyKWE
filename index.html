How your money works at KW Explore

Transparent. Capped. Agent-focused.

1. Split & cap

You are on 70/30 for sales and 60/40 for rental placements until you have paid R140 000 to the market centre (your annual cap).

Rentals contribute towards your cap. You do not cap on rentals themselves.

2. After you cap

Once capped, your sales commission moves to 100% for the balance of your anniversary year.

  • • R1 500 transaction fee per sales transaction.
  • • Rentals remain at 60/40.
  • • Royalties (6%) and Growthshare (2%) still apply.

3. Beyond your own deals

When you help grow the company by attracting productive agents, you can earn Growthshare on their production – a long-term, leveraged income line.

This sits on top of your personal sales and rental income.

Simple example (illustrative)

If you close ±2 sales per month at your current average commission:

  • • You reach your cap, then keep 100% (less fees) on sales for the rest of your year.
  • • Rentals you place keep contributing to your cap and your monthly cash flow.
  • • Any agents you introduce that join and produce can add Growthshare on top.

Registration, BOC and compliance support

To trade legally as an estate agent in South Africa you must be registered with the PPRA and hold a valid Fidelity Fund Certificate (FFC). At KW Explore you are not left to figure this out alone – we walk you through each step.

Intern Property Practitioner process (new to the industry)

For agents entering the industry or without a current FFC. We help you move from zero to fully registered, productive intern.

Step 1

Join KW Explore

Sign your independent contractor agreement, choose your launch date and meet the leadership team.

Step 2

BOC & PPRA intern application

We assist with your intern registration: PPRA forms, supporting documents, proof of payment and your employment letter from the Principal.

Step 3

FFC issued & supervised trading

Once your intern FFC is issued you are compliant to trade as an intern property practitioner under supervision, using our systems and models.

Step 4

Logbook, NQF4 & full status

We support you with logbook, NQF4 qualification and the final PPRA upgrade so you move from intern to full-status agent.

Full status agent process (existing FFC / qualified)

For agents who already hold an FFC or have completed the required qualifications. We make the move to KW Explore structured and low-friction.

Step 1

Business & numbers consult

We review your current production, goals and financial model and design your onboarding and launch plan.

Step 2

Change of agency / BOC process

We handle the PPRA change of employment documentation, BOC updates and internal onboarding paperwork with you.

Step 3

Updated FFC & system access

Your FFC is updated to KW Explore and you are onboarded onto our tech stack, templates and support structures.

Step 4

Launch & growth plan

Together with leadership and your productivity coach we build your 90–180 day launch and growth plan inside KW Explore.

Joining checklist (once you say yes)

  1. Complete the joining document on the link we send you.
  2. Pay the R3 500 joining fee (includes 1 month BOC) and send proof of payment to the MCA.
  3. Send supporting documents: ID/Passport, Proof of Bank, Proof of Address and a profile photo.
  4. Onboarding session with DFI/MCTT – bring your laptop and phone so we can set up your systems and tools.

You will know exactly who to send what to, with clear emails and WhatsApp details in your welcome pack.

Whether you are starting out as an intern or arriving as a full-status agent, you focus on production while we help you stay compliant, registered and on track with your career path.
